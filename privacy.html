Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Pickup Soccer ("the App") is operated by Alex Dvornikov ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

What we collect

  • Name — to identify you in the team roster and team sheets
  • Email address — used for authentication (login) only
  • Profile photo — if provided by your Google or Facebook login, used as your avatar
  • Position preferences — the positions you prefer to play (GK, DEF, MID, FWD)
  • Game attendance records — which games you attended, which team you were on

We do not collect your phone number, location, financial information, or any sensitive personal data.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use cookies beyond what is necessary for authentication
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not show you ads

How we use your information

  • To display your name and position on team sheets visible to other group members
  • To balance teams fairly based on skill level (skill ratings are only visible to group admins and captains — never to other players)
  • To keep a history of past games for your group
  • To authenticate your login via Google, Facebook, or email/password

Who can see your information

  • Your name and position — visible to all members of your group and anyone with the link to the team page
  • Your skill rating — visible only to group admins and captains, never to other players or the public
  • Your email address — visible only to the group admin, never displayed publicly

Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — web hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — optional login method. Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Login — optional login method. Meta Privacy Policy

When you use Google or Facebook to log in, those services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your group is active and you are a member. If you request deletion of your account, your personal information will be removed within 30 days.

Your rights

  • Access — you can request a copy of your personal data at any time
  • Correction — you can update your name and position preferences in the app
  • Deletion — you can request full deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — you can request an export of your data

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
