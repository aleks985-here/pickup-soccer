Data Deletion Request

How to request deletion of your personal data from Pickup Soccer

You have the right to request deletion of all personal data we hold about you. This includes your name, email address, position preferences, and game history records.

We will process all deletion requests within 30 days and send you a confirmation email when complete.

How to request deletion

1
Send an email to [email protected] Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request — Pickup Soccer"
2
Include your information Your name as it appears in the app, and the email address you used to sign in (Google, Facebook, or email/password).
3
We confirm and delete We will reply within 5 business days to confirm receipt, and complete the deletion within 30 days. You will receive a final confirmation when done.

What gets deleted

Upon a confirmed deletion request, we will remove:

  1. Your authentication account (email, login method)
  2. Your player profile (name, position preferences, skill rating)
  3. Your attendance records from game history
  4. Any role assignments (captain, admin)

What we cannot delete

Game results and team compositions from past games may be retained in anonymized form (team scores, number of players) as part of group history. Your name will be removed from all team records.

If you signed in with Facebook

You can also revoke the app's access to your Facebook account directly from Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook account Settings
  2. Click Security and Login → Apps and Websites
  3. Find Pickup Soccer → click Remove

Revoking Facebook access removes the login connection. To also delete your data from our servers, please send us an email as described above.
